What is KIWS?

KIWS stands for Kids weekend School, It is a training program for kids and young individuals on different programs. Namely; MUSIC, CODING, GRAPHICS, SWIMMING and DANCING

What is the fee?

The Promo price for the KIWS is 20,000 naira Monthly and a one time Registration fee of 5,000 naira.
